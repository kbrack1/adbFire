Multiple Devices
adbFire has the capability to manage up to ten Fire TV devices. Simply select one of the combobox items (1-10) and then click The Setup Device button to define the preferences for that device.

Each device has individual preferences, including media software installed, video cache settings, storage location for pulled files & screenshots, ssh password (if rooted) and more.

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbFire produces a log file, adbfire.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
