adbFire v1.14 is a companion program for Android Kodi and its forks. It also has many special features for the AFTV.

adbFire allows you to install (sideload) Kodi and other apps to your Android device without the bother of installing the Android SDK.

Additionally, it allows you to mount USB drives (when rooted) copy files to and from Kodi, backup and restore Kodi's data, root the AFTV (51.1.1.0 or earlier) and much more.

For Windows XP and up, the adbFire folder can be located wherever you wish.

Under OS X, adbFire.app may also be located wherever you wish.

adbFire keeps two log files adbfire.log and adbfire.old.log. Please use the View Log menu function to see your logs and to copy them to the clipboard for tech support.

The adb binaries and dlls are part of the Android SDK.

adbFire depends on these apps:

com.geohot.towelroot
eu.chainfire.supersu
com.kebab.llama
com.funkyfresh.samba
CWM Recovery
