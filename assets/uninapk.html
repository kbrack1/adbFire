The "Uninstall APK" button uninstalls non-system apps from your Android device. Double-click or highlight apps then click "ok" to select. To retain application data tick the "-k" checkbox. Data retention has only been tested with Kodi.

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbFire produces a log file, adbfire.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
