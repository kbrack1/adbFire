USB Mode
adbFire features USB Mode to manage Kodi. USB Mode is available for all devices.

To use USB Mode with the Stick, connect your Fire TV Stick to your PC via USB Cable.

To use USB Mode on the Fire TV, under the Fire TV's Settings-System->Developer Options, enable USB Debugging and connect your Fire TV to your PC via USB Cable. This will disable USB Storage.

Click the USB Mode checkbox to connect; you may have to click the checkbox a few times. Once connected a popup dialog will ask you to choose tour media center software. Once selected, all the Kodi functions will be available.

In rare cases you may need to use adbFire's Console to establish a USB connection. If so, once you are connected and not before, click the USB via console checkbox. A popup dialog will ask you to choose tour media center software. Once selected, all the Kodi functions will be available.

Please note: Currently only one device at a time can be connected via USB or IP.

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbFire produces a log file, adbfire.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
