"Root AFTV" The "Root AFTV" button installs Chainfire Superuser and GeoHot's towelroot v 3.0. You must complete the process by running the towelroot app. Unfortunately, firmware 51.1.3.0 and above can't be rooted at this time. Check your Fire TV Settings/About to see your firmware ID.

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbFire produces a log file, adbfire.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
